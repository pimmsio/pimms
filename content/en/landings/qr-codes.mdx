::: faq
### What's the difference between static and dynamic QR codes?
Static QR codes point to a fixed URL — you can't change it. Dynamic QR codes redirect to a URL you control, so you can update the destination anytime. PIMMS creates dynamic QR codes so you never have to reprint.
:::

::: faq
### Can I track scans with QR codes?
Yes. Every PIMMS QR code tracks scans in real time. You'll see scan counts, devices, locations, and conversion attribution in your dashboard.
:::

::: faq
### Can I customize the appearance of my QR code?
Yes. Add your logo, change colors, and adjust the design. PIMMS QR codes support custom branding while staying scannable.
:::

::: faq
### Do QR codes work with deep links?
Yes. PIMMS QR codes use smart links, which include deep linking. When someone scans on mobile, they can be sent to the app store, a product page, or a booking flow — not stuck in an in-app browser.
:::
