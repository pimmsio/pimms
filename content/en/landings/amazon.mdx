::: faq
### What makes PIMMS different from a regular Amazon link?
When you share a link to a product or book on Amazon, it often opens in a small in-app browser (like Instagram or Facebook). PIMMS redirects users straight to the official Amazon app. The result? A smoother shopping experience, more sales, and more positive reviews.
:::

::: faq
### Can I track who clicks on my Amazon links?
Yes. You'll see how many people clicked, where they came from (social networks, newsletters, etc.), and what they did next (add to cart, purchase, etc.). Even on the free plan, you get clear, detailed insights.
:::

::: faq
### Can PIMMS help me increase sales?
Absolutely. By sending shoppers directly to the official Amazon app (instead of a web browser), you reduce friction. Customers are already signed in to their Amazon account, so it's just one tap to buy or leave a review. Fewer steps mean higher conversions.
:::

::: faq
### Can I create a link for each product or book?

:::

::: faq
### Can I see which links perform best?
Yes. You can see how many people clicked each link and where they came from (platform, device, country, etc.). While PIMMS doesn't track every detail of a purchase, this info lets you see which channels truly bring in conversions.
:::

::: faq
### Does PIMMS also work on other platforms?
