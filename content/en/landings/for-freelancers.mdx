::: faq
### Is PIMMS free?
Yes. The free plan includes conversion tracking — connect Stripe, Shopify, Cal.com, Tally, and more. 5 smart links, 200 events/month. No credit card required.
:::

::: faq
### Do I need a developer?
No. PIMMS is built for no-code setup. Add a script to your site or use Link Magnet — most solo founders are up and running in under 15 minutes.
:::

::: faq
### Can I track Stripe payments?
Yes. One-click connect. Every sale is attributed to the link, UTM, and campaign that drove it. Works on the free plan.
:::

::: faq
### What's the lifetime deal?
Tiny: €79 once. Solo: €129 once. No recurring fees. Both include conversion tracking, Stripe/Shopify, and smart links. Ideal for freelancers who want to pay once and forget.
:::

::: faq
### How is this different from Bitly?
Bitly tracks clicks only. PIMMS tracks clicks, leads, and revenue. You see which LinkedIn post, email, or campaign actually drove that Stripe payment — full conversion attribution, not just vanity metrics.
