### How does the Webflow integration work?
PIMMS connects to your Webflow site via a webhook for form submissions. When a visitor submits a form on your Webflow site, the submission is sent to PIMMS with full UTM attribution. You'll see exactly which link, campaign, or post drove each lead‚Äîno manual tracking needed.
:::

::: faq
### How simple is the setup?
Enable Link Magnet with one toggle for out-of-the-box tracking. For full-site tracking, add one script. Most setups take 10‚Äì15 minutes, no developer required.
:::

::: faq
### What can I track beyond clicks?
Track clicks, leads, and revenue. Connect forms, calendars, and payments (Stripe, Tally, Typeform, Cal.com, Calendly, Shopify) to tie each conversion to its original link and UTM.
:::

::: faq
### How does UTM tracking work in PIMMS?
Create UTM templates and parameters, enforce naming rules, bulk-generate links, and organize campaigns in folders. Filter and analyze by source, medium, campaign, term, and content.
:::
