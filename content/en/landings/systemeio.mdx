::: faq
### How does PIMMS integrate with Systeme.io?
PIMMS connects via webhooks to track every opt-in, sale, and upsell in your Systeme.io funnels. You'll see which campaigns drive revenue in real-time, with full attribution from first click to final purchase.
:::

::: faq
### Will this slow down my Systeme.io funnels?
No. PIMMS uses server-side tracking that doesn't affect your funnel performance. The integration is lightweight and runs in the background without impacting user experience.
:::

::: faq
### Can I track email sequences individually?
Yes. Generate unique smart links for each email in your sequences. See which emails drive the most conversions and optimize your messaging based on real revenue data.
:::

::: faq
### Does it work with Systeme.io's built-in email marketing?
Absolutely. Whether you use Systeme.io's email builder or integrate with external tools like Brevo or Mailchimp, PIMMS tracks all touchpoints that lead to funnel conversions.
:::

::: faq
### How accurate is the attribution for mobile traffic?
Extremely accurate. PIMMS uses server-side tracking with privacy-safe IDs that work across domains and apps. Attribution remains consistent even on iOS and with ad blockers enabled.
:::

::: faq
### Can I A/B test my Systeme.io funnels?
Yes. Create multiple versions of your landing pages or email sequences, and PIMMS will show you which variants generate more revenue. Perfect for optimizing conversion rates.
:::

::: faq
### What about GDPR compliance?
PIMMS is privacy-first by design. No third-party cookies, only first-party tracking. You control data retention and integrations. Fully compliant with GDPR and privacy regulations.
:::

::: faq
### How long does setup take?
10-15 minutes total. Paste one script into your Systeme.io settings, create a webhook, and start using smart links. No developers needed, no complex configuration.
:::

::: faq
### Can I track affiliate campaigns through Systeme.io?
Yes. PIMMS tracks affiliate referrals, influencer campaigns, and partner promotions. See which affiliates drive the most valuable customers to your Systeme.io funnels.
:::

::: faq
### What happens to my existing Systeme.io analytics?
Nothing changes. PIMMS adds attribution on top of your existing setup. You keep all your current Systeme.io reports while gaining deeper insights into what's driving your revenue.
:::
