    <H2>Frequently asked questions</H2>

::: faq
### Is PIMMS really free to start?
Yes! The Free plan includes 5 links, 200 tracked events per month, and unlimited sales tracking. No credit card required — you can upgrade anytime.
:::

::: faq
### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. All payments are processed securely.
:::

::: faq
### How safe is card payment?
We use Stripe to handle all payments. Your card details are encrypted end-to-end and never stored on our servers. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of security.
:::

::: faq
### What happens when I upgrade or downgrade?
When you upgrade, the new plan features are applied immediately. You only pay the prorated difference for the rest of your billing cycle. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
:::

::: faq
### What if I bought a lifetime plan and want to upgrade?
Simple — you just pay the price difference between your current lifetime plan and the one you want to upgrade to. No complicated calculations, no wasted money.
:::

::: faq
### Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period, then you'll be moved to the Free plan.
:::

::: faq
### Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of a paid plan, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
:::

::: faq
### Can I use PIMMS for commercial purposes?
Yes, all plans — including the Free plan — can be used for commercial purposes. PIMMS is built for marketers, creators, and businesses of all sizes.
:::

::: faq
### Do you have any hidden fees?
No hidden fees, ever. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no setup costs, no overage charges, and no surprises.
:::

::: faq
### Can I export my links and data?
Yes. You can export your links and analytics data at any time. Analytics CSV export is available on the Solo plan and above.
:::
