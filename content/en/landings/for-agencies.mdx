::: faq
### Can I manage multiple clients?
Yes. Use link folders to organize by client and campaign. Add multiple custom domains — one per client — so each client has links branded to their image. Filter analytics by folder to see performance per client.
:::

::: faq
### How many team members can I add?
Up to 10 members on the Business plan. Invite your account managers and media buyers. Each member gets access to the same links and dashboards. Pro includes 3 seats.
:::

::: faq
### Can clients see their own data?
Yes. Share read-only dashboard links filtered to a specific folder or client. Clients see clicks, leads and revenue for their campaigns — with no access to other clients or account settings.
:::

::: faq
### Do you offer agency pricing?
Pro ($39/month or $390/year) and Business ($69/month or $690/year) are built for agencies. Annual billing saves 2 months. Contact us for larger teams or full white-label.
:::

::: faq
### Can I white-label links?
Yes. Add a custom domain per client (e.g. links.clientbrand.com). Links use the client's brand. Dashboards display PIMMS branding unless you use our Enterprise plan for full white-label.
:::
