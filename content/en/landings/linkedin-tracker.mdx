::: faq
### How simple is the setup?
Enable Link Magnet with one toggle for out-of-the-box tracking. For full-site tracking, add one script. Most setups take 10–15 minutes, no developer required.
:::

::: faq
### What can I track beyond clicks?
Track clicks, leads, and revenue. Connect forms, calendars, and payments (Stripe, Tally, Typeform, Cal.com, Calendly, Shopify) to tie each conversion to its original link and UTM.
:::

::: faq
### How does UTM tracking work in PIMMS?
Create UTM templates and parameters, enforce naming rules, bulk-generate links, and organize campaigns in folders. Filter and analyze by source, medium, campaign, term, and content.
:::

::: faq
### Can I keep my current tools and workflow?
Yes. Use native integrations plus Zapier, Make, webhooks, and API to plug into your CRM, email, and analytics stack.
:::

::: faq
### Does PIMMS work across domains and subdomains?
Yes. Install the script on each domain/subdomain you control to unify attribution. Links can point anywhere.
:::

::: faq
### What’s included in the free plan?
5 smart links, 200 tracked events/month, 30-day analytics retention, 3 UTM templates, and 10 saved UTM parameters. No credit card required.
:::

::: faq
### What are the paid plans?

:::

::: faq
### Do you have an API and webhooks?
Yes — a full REST API and webhooks for custom workflows and analytics exports.
:::
