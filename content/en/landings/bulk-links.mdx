::: faq
### How does CSV import work?
Paste a list of URLs or upload a CSV file. PIMMS creates tracked links for each URL, applies your chosen UTM template, and optionally assigns them to a folder. You can import dozens of links in one go.
:::

::: faq
### Can I organize links in folders?
Yes. Create folders for campaigns, clients, or channels. Move links between folders, apply bulk operations to entire folders, and filter your dashboard by folder.
:::

::: faq
### Can I apply UTM templates in bulk?
Yes. Select multiple links or an entire folder, choose a UTM template, and apply it to all selected links. Keeps your Google Analytics data clean and consistent.
:::

::: faq
### What bulk operations are available?
Edit destinations, apply UTM templates, move to folders, archive, and export. Most actions can be applied to multiple links or entire folders at once.
:::

::: faq
### Is bulk link management included in the free plan?
Yes. CSV import, link folders, and bulk UTM are available on all plans. The free plan includes 5 smart links. Upgrade for more links as you scale.
:::
