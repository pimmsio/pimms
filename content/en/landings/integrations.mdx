::: faq
### How long does it take to set up an integration?
Most integrations (Stripe, Cal.com, Tally) take less than 5 minutes. Just connect your account and PIMMS starts tracking automatically.
:::

::: faq
### Is Stripe tracking really free?
Yes. Stripe and Shopify conversion tracking is included in the free plan. No credit card required.
:::

::: faq
### Can I use Zapier to connect PIMMS to my CRM?
Yes. PIMMS is available on the Zapier marketplace. Push clicks, leads, and sales events to HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Airtable, Slack, and 5,000+ more apps.
:::

::: faq
### Do I need to install an SDK?
No. Most integrations work via webhooks or native connections — no code required. For advanced use cases, we provide a TypeScript SDK and REST API.
:::

::: faq
### Which analytics tools work with PIMMS?
PIMMS works alongside Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, and any tool that reads UTM parameters. Use both for complementary insights.
