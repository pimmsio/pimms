::: faq
### What can I track beyond clicks?
PIMMS tracks three layers: clicks (with geo, device, UTM breakdowns), leads (from forms and calendars with source attribution), and revenue (Stripe and Shopify payments attributed to campaigns).
:::

::: faq
### Is conversion tracking really free?
Yes. Stripe and Shopify conversion tracking is included in the free plan. Most competitors charge $75+/month for similar features.
:::

::: faq
### How does server-side tracking work?
PIMMS captures events server-side, so ad blockers and cookie restrictions don't affect your data. Every click, lead, and sale is recorded accurately.
:::

::: faq
### Which form and calendar tools are supported?
Brevo, Tally, Typeform, Cal.com, Calendly, Webflow forms, Framer forms, Elementor forms, Podia, iClosed, and more. Each submission is automatically linked to its original UTM source.
:::

::: faq
### Can I export my data?
Yes. Use webhooks to push events in real time, the API for custom queries, or integrate with Zapier to connect to 5,000+ apps.
:::
