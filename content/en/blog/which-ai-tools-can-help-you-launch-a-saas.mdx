For SaaS businesses, ChatGPT's seamless integration options can improve workflows and strengthen security. To mitigate such risks, SaaS founders should establish strict governance policies and treat these integrations as high-risk connections requiring strong security measures [\[8\]](https://www.metomic.io/resource-centre/how-is-chatgpts-saas-integration-exposing-enterprise-data-to-unprecedented-risk).
