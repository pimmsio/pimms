**Framer form tracking** lets you see exactly which marketing campaigns generate your best leads and form submissions from your Framer website. The Pimms dashboard has an **interactive setup wizard** that handles all technical details for you.

You'll get **real-time form analytics** showing which ads, YouTube posts, or email campaigns drive actual form submissions. Perfect for **Framer lead generation** with complete **website analytics** from click to conversion.

Framer is one of the most popular website builders for startups and solo founders. With Pimms, you can track every form submission on your Framer site and know exactly where each lead came from — no developer needed.

## Why track Framer forms with Pimms?

- **Find out which landing page converts best.** You built three landing pages in Framer, each targeting a different audience. Pimms shows you which page generates the most form submissions — and from which traffic source — so you can focus on what works.

- **Prove that your content marketing drives leads.** You share blog posts and guides that link to your Framer site. Without Pimms, you see form submissions but can't connect them to specific content. With Pimms, every form fill is attributed to the article or post that brought the visitor.

- **Measure paid vs organic lead quality.** You run Google Ads and also get organic traffic from SEO. Both generate form submissions. Pimms shows you which source produces more leads — and you can see the visitor's full journey from click to form submission.

## What you'll track

- **Framer form submissions** — Every form fill across your entire Framer site, with the visitor's details
- **Lead attribution by campaign** — Which ad, email, social post, or content piece drove each form submission
- **Campaign performance** — Conversion rates and lead counts broken down by traffic source
- **Multi-form analytics** — Track all forms across all pages from one dashboard

## How to set it up

Go to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io), open **Integrations → Framer**, and launch the setup wizard. It walks you through adding two scripts to your Framer site, building a compatible form, configuring the webhook, and verifying with a test submission. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

## What the setup wizard covers

The Framer wizard has 6 steps:

1. **Install tracking scripts** — Detection script (site-wide) and form injection script (on pages with forms)
2. **Verify script installation** — Confirm both scripts are loading correctly
3. **Build a Pimms-compatible form** — Follow the form field requirements to ensure tracking works
4. **Configure Framer webhook** — Connect form submissions to Pimms
5. **Create a test link** — Generate a tracking link to your Framer page
6. **Verify tracking works** — Submit a test form to confirm everything is connected

## How it works

1. **Install the Pimms scripts** on your Framer site — the detection script identifies visitors from Pimms links, and the form injection script adds a hidden tracking field to your forms
2. **Create a Pimms link** pointing to your Framer page (e.g. `pim.ms/get-started`)
3. **A visitor clicks your link**, lands on your Framer site, and fills out a form
4. **Framer sends a webhook** to Pimms with the form data and tracking ID, and Pimms attributes the lead to the original campaign

The form injection script works automatically with Framer's native form components — no manual field configuration needed.

## What you'll see in your dashboard

Your Pimms dashboard shows every Framer form submission in real time with full campaign attribution. For each lead, you'll see the form data (name, email, message), which Pimms link they clicked, from which platform, and which campaign. Filter by date, traffic source, or form to understand which channels and pages generate the most leads.

## FAQs

::: faq

### Does Framer form tracking work with free plans?

No, Framer form tracking requires a paid Framer plan to access custom code features. The free Framer plan doesn't allow adding tracking scripts needed for form analytics and lead attribution. Most users upgrade to the Basic plan for tracking capabilities.

:::

::: faq

### Can I track multiple forms across my Framer website?

Yes! Once you install the Pimms tracking scripts, all forms across your entire Framer website are automatically tracked. Each form submission gets attributed to the specific campaign, ad, or channel that brought the visitor, giving you complete website analytics visibility.

:::

::: faq

### Does this work with Framer's drag-and-drop form components?

Absolutely! Framer form tracking works seamlessly with all of Framer's native form components and templates. The webhook integration automatically captures submissions from any form component you drag onto your pages, no additional configuration needed.

:::

::: faq

### Can I use Framer form tracking together with Cal.com or Stripe?

Yes. If you embed Cal.com on your Framer site or link to Stripe checkout, you can set up multiple integrations. The detection script works with all Pimms integrations — just make sure to follow each integration's wizard in the dashboard.

:::
