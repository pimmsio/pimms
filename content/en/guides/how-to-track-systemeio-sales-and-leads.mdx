**Systeme.io sales tracking** lets you see exactly which marketing campaigns generate actual revenue across your entire funnel system. This complete guide shows you how to set up **Systeme.io funnel analytics** and **conversion tracking** in 10-15 minutes using Pimms.

You'll get **real-time sales attribution** showing which ads, email campaigns, or social media posts drive actual purchases through multi-step funnels. Perfect for **Systeme.io revenue analytics** with complete **customer journey tracking** from first click to final sale.

**What you'll accomplish:** Complete sales tracking setup, webhook integration, and revenue attribution for every funnel in your Systeme.io account.

## 1. Install the tracking script

1. Go to your **Systeme.io dashboard**

![Paste Pimms script in footer settings](https://assets.pimms.io/systemeio-footer-code-settings.webp?v=2)

2. Open your **Profile Settings**
3. In the left menu, select **Sales funnels**
4. Paste this script into the **Tracking code** field:

```html
<script defer src="https://cdn.pimms.io/analytics/script.detection.js" data-forward-all="true"></script>
```

5. Click **Save**

## 2. Find your Workspace ID

1. Log in to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io)
2. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Workspace ID**
3. Copy your ID (e.g. `ws_abc123xyz`)

## 3. Add the webhook

1. In Systeme.io, go to your profile ‚Üí **Settings**
2. Scroll down to **Webhooks**
3. Click **Create**
4. Set it up like this:
- **Name**: Pimms Webhook  
- **Webhook URL**:  

```
https://app.pimms.io/api/systemeio/webhook?workspace_id=your-id
```

*(Replace `your-id` with your actual Workspace ID)*  
- **Secret**: Your Workspace ID  
- **Events**:  
  ‚úÖ `Opt-in`  
  ‚úÖ `New sale`

![Systeme.io Webhook Settings](https://assets.pimms.io/systemeio-webhook-settings.webp)

‚ö†Ô∏è Make sure the URL and Secret both use the exact same Workspace ID.

## 4. Create a tracking link in Pimms

1. Go to [Pimms](https://app.pimms.io)
2. Create a new short link pointing to the **first step** of your Systeme.io funnel
3. Enable **Conversion tracking**

**Example**:  
```
https://pim.ms/my-sales-funnel
```

Use this link in your ads, emails, and social channels‚Äîeverywhere you get traffic.

![Pimms short link](https://assets.pimms.io/systemeio-create-short-link.webp)

## 5. Test your setup

1. Open your Pimms link in an **incognito tab**
2. Go through your funnel (opt-in + sale if applicable)
3. Use a test email (like `test+pimms@yourdomain.com`)
4. Check your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io)

You should see a new lead and, if applicable, a new sale‚Äîboth with full attribution.

![Pimms lead and sale recorded](https://assets.pimms.io/systemeio-dashboard-lead-sale.webp)

## 6. Troubleshooting tips

- Confirm that the Pimms script is saved in your funnel settings
- Double-check that your **Workspace ID** is the same in both the webhook URL and the secret
- Test in **incognito mode** with a fresh email address
- Check the webhook logs in Systeme.io to verify that events were triggered
- Make sure visitors enter your funnel via your **Pimms link**, not directly

## FAQs

::: faq

### How do I track sales across multiple Systeme.io funnels?

Systeme.io sales tracking with Pimms automatically works across all your funnels once configured. Each sale gets attributed to the specific campaign that brought the customer, whether they convert through Funnel A or Funnel B. You'll see complete revenue attribution data showing which traffic sources generate sales across your entire funnel ecosystem.

:::

::: faq

### Does Systeme.io sales tracking work with affiliate programs?

Yes! Systeme.io sales tracking works perfectly with affiliate programs. You can create unique Pimms links for each affiliate, and when they generate sales through any of your funnels, you'll see exactly which affiliate drove the revenue. This gives you complete affiliate performance analytics beyond basic click tracking.

:::

::: faq

### How quickly do I see sales data in my analytics dashboard?

Sales data appears in your Pimms dashboard within 1-2 minutes after the Systeme.io webhook fires. You'll see real-time revenue attribution including the sale amount, product purchased, customer details, and the complete campaign journey that led to the purchase.

:::
