**Systeme.io sales tracking** lets you see exactly which marketing campaigns generate actual revenue across your entire funnel system. The Pimms dashboard includes an interactive Systeme.io setup wizard that handles all technical details for you.

Whether you sell online courses, coaching programs, or digital products, connecting Systeme.io to Pimms gives you complete **funnel attribution** — from the first click on your ad or social post all the way through to the completed purchase.

Systeme.io is an all-in-one platform used by thousands of entrepreneurs and small businesses. With Pimms, you finally get the attribution data that Systeme.io's built-in analytics don't provide: **which specific campaign brought each customer**.

## Why track Systeme.io with Pimms?

- **Find out which traffic source actually sells.** You promote your funnel on YouTube, in Facebook groups, and through email. Without Pimms, you see opt-ins and sales in Systeme.io but don't know which channel drove them. With Pimms, every sale and opt-in is linked to the exact campaign that generated it.

- **Measure affiliate performance with real data.** You have 10 affiliates promoting your Systeme.io products. Give each one a unique Pimms link and see exactly who drives opt-ins and who drives actual sales — not just who sends the most clicks.

- **Optimize multi-step funnels.** You run a webinar funnel: ad → opt-in → replay → sales page. Pimms tracks the entire journey, so you can see which ad creative generates not just opt-ins but actual purchases at the end of the funnel.

- **Systeme.io sales** — Every completed purchase with product name, amount, and customer email

- **Funnel analytics** — Track performance across all your Systeme.io funnels from one dashboard

## How to set it up

Go to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io), open **Integrations → Systeme.io**, and launch the setup wizard. It walks you through adding the tracking script to your Systeme.io settings, configuring the webhook for opt-in and sale events, and verifying with a test submission. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

## What the setup wizard covers

The Systeme.io wizard has 5 steps:

1. **Install the tracking script** — Add the Pimms detection script to Systeme.io's tracking code settings
2. **Verify script installation** — Confirm the script is loading on your funnel pages
3. **Configure webhook** — Set up webhooks for opt-in and new sale events
4. **Create a test link** — Generate a tracking link to your Systeme.io funnel page
5. **Verify tracking works** — Complete a test opt-in to confirm attribution is connected

## How it works

1. **Install the Pimms detection script** in Systeme.io's global tracking code settings — it runs on all your funnel pages and forwards attribution data through every step
2. **Create a Pimms link** pointing to your Systeme.io funnel page (e.g. `pim.ms/webinar-signup`)
3. **A visitor clicks your link**, enters your funnel, opts in, and potentially makes a purchase
4. **Systeme.io fires a webhook** to Pimms for each event (opt-in and sale), and Pimms attributes each conversion to the original campaign

The `data-forward-all` script attribute ensures that attribution data flows through every page of your multi-step funnel — from landing page to opt-in to checkout.

## What you'll see in your dashboard

Your Pimms dashboard shows every Systeme.io opt-in and sale in real time with full campaign attribution. For each conversion, you'll see the customer's email, the event type (opt-in or sale), the product and amount (for sales), and the complete trail — which Pimms link they clicked, from which platform, and which campaign. Filter by date, channel, or funnel to compare performance.

## FAQs

::: faq

### How do I track sales across multiple Systeme.io funnels?

Systeme.io sales tracking with Pimms automatically works across all your funnels once configured. Each sale gets attributed to the specific campaign that brought the customer, whether they convert through Funnel A or Funnel B. You'll see complete revenue attribution data showing which traffic sources generate sales across your entire funnel ecosystem.

:::

::: faq

### Does Systeme.io sales tracking work with affiliate programs?

Yes! Systeme.io sales tracking works perfectly with affiliate programs. You can create unique Pimms links for each affiliate, and when they generate sales through any of your funnels, you'll see exactly which affiliate drove the revenue. This gives you complete affiliate performance analytics beyond basic click tracking.

:::

::: faq

### How quickly do I see sales data in my analytics dashboard?

Sales data appears in your Pimms dashboard within 1-2 minutes after the Systeme.io webhook fires. You'll see real-time revenue attribution including the sale amount, product purchased, customer details, and the complete campaign journey that led to the purchase.

:::

::: faq

### Does the tracking work across multi-step funnels?

Yes. The Pimms detection script uses a special `data-forward-all` attribute that carries attribution data through every page of your Systeme.io funnel. Whether your funnel has 2 steps or 10 steps, the original traffic source is preserved all the way to the final conversion.

:::
