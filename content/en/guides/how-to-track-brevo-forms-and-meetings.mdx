## 1. Find your Workspace ID

## 2. Create your entry link

This is the link you share in ads, emails, LinkedIn, etc.

1. Go to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io)
2. Create a short link pointing to your **Brevo Form page** or **Brevo Meeting booking page**

**Example**:
```

```

## 3. Create your thank-you link

This is the redirect Brevo sends users to after a submission/booking. It lets Pimms record the conversion and attribute it to the entry link.

1. Go to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io)
2. Create a short link whose key ends with `/thankyou`
3. Set the destination to **any** thank-you URL you want

**Example**:
```
https://pim.ms/abc/thankyou
```

Tip: you can use the same thank-you link for both Forms and Meetings, or create one per flow.

## 5. Set the thank-you URL

This is what connects attribution (session) to the conversion (submission/booking).

- **Brevo Forms**: set the form **thank-you URL** to your Pimms thank-you link (step 3)
- **Brevo Meetings**: set the meeting **confirmation/thank-you redirect URL** to your Pimms thank-you link (step 3)

## 6. Test your setup

1. Open your **entry Pimms link** in an incognito tab
2. Submit the Brevo form or book a Brevo meeting with a test email
3. Ensure you land on the final thank-you page (via your Pimms thank-you link)
4. Check your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io)

You should see a new conversion with attribution.

## 7. Troubleshooting

### I see webhooks in Brevo, but no conversions in Pimms
- Make sure Brevo redirects to your **Pimms thank-you link** (not directly to the thank-you page)
- Make sure you start the session via the **entry Pimms link**

### The webhook works, but Iâ€™m missing email/name
- Make sure your Brevo form / meeting collects the right fields:
  - An email field whose name contains `email`
  - A name field whose name contains `name`, or two fields: `firstname` + `lastname`
- If you renamed fields, keep these keywords in the field names so Pimms can map them.
