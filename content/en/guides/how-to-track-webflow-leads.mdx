**Webflow form tracking** lets you see exactly which marketing campaigns generate your best leads and form submissions. The Pimms dashboard has an **interactive setup wizard** that handles all technical details for you.

You'll get **real-time conversion tracking** showing which ads, YouTube posts, or email campaigns drive actual form submissions. Perfect for **Webflow lead generation** with complete **marketing attribution** across all your website forms.

Webflow is one of the most popular tools for building marketing sites, landing pages, and lead-gen funnels. With Pimms, you can track every form submission and know exactly which campaign brought each lead — no code changes to your Webflow forms needed.

## Why track Webflow forms with Pimms?

- **Know which landing page converts best.** You built separate Webflow pages for different campaigns (e.g. one for Google Ads, one for a partnership). Pimms shows you which page generates the most form submissions from each source — so you can invest in the highest-converting combination.

- **Measure SEO-driven leads.** Your Webflow blog ranks for several keywords. Visitors read articles and fill out contact forms. Pimms connects each form submission to the original search query and page, showing you which content actually generates leads.

- **Track agency or client campaign performance.** Running campaigns for clients on their Webflow sites? Create separate Pimms links per campaign and show clients exactly how many leads each campaign generated — with data, not estimates.

## What you'll track

- **Campaign performance** — Conversion rates and lead counts broken down by traffic source
- **Multi-page analytics** — Track forms across all pages from one dashboard

## How to set it up

Go to your [Pimms dashboard](https://app.pimms.io), open **Integrations → Webflow**, and launch the setup wizard. It walks you through adding two scripts to your Webflow site, building a compatible form, configuring the webhook, and verifying with a test submission. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

## What the setup wizard covers

The Webflow wizard has 6 steps:

1. **Install tracking scripts** — Detection script and form injection script
2. **Verify script installation** — Confirm both scripts are loading correctly
3. **Build a Pimms-compatible form** — Follow the form field requirements to ensure tracking works
4. **Configure Webflow webhook** — Connect form submissions to Pimms via Webflow's webhook settings
5. **Create a test link** — Generate a tracking link to your Webflow page
6. **Verify tracking works** — Submit a test form to confirm everything is connected

## How it works

1. **Install the Pimms scripts** on your Webflow site — the detection script identifies visitors from Pimms links, and the form injection script adds a hidden tracking field to your forms
2. **Create a Pimms link** pointing to your Webflow page (e.g. `pim.ms/contact-us`)
3. **A visitor clicks your link**, lands on your Webflow site, and fills out a form
4. **Webflow sends a webhook** to Pimms with the form data and tracking ID, and Pimms attributes the lead to the original campaign

The form injection script works automatically with Webflow's form components — it adds a hidden `pimms_id` field that captures the visitor's attribution data.

## What you'll see in your dashboard

Your Pimms dashboard shows every Webflow form submission in real time with full campaign attribution. For each lead, you'll see the form data (name, email, message), which Pimms link they clicked, from which platform, and which campaign. Filter by date, traffic source, or page to understand which channels generate the most leads.

## FAQs

::: faq

### How long does Webflow form tracking setup take?

Complete Webflow form tracking setup takes a few minutes using the interactive wizard in the Pimms dashboard. The wizard handles script installation, webhook configuration, and verification. Most users complete the setup in one session with immediate tracking results.

:::

::: faq

### Does Webflow form tracking work with free plans?

No, Webflow form tracking requires a paid Webflow plan to access custom code features. The free Webflow plan doesn't allow adding tracking scripts or custom code needed for form analytics and lead attribution.

:::

::: faq

### Can I track multiple forms on one Webflow site?

Yes! Once you configure Pimms tracking, all forms across your entire Webflow site are automatically tracked. Each form submission gets attributed to the specific campaign, ad, or channel that brought the visitor, giving you complete form analytics visibility.

:::

::: faq

### Does Webflow tracking work with Webflow CMS collections?

Yes. The tracking scripts work on all Webflow pages, including CMS collection pages. If you have forms on dynamic pages (like individual blog posts or service pages), they'll all be tracked with full attribution.

:::
