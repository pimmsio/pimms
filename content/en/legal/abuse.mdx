PIMMS is committed to preventing abuse and misuse of its platform to create malicious short links, including but not limited to the following:

- Phishing or scam websites
- Pornography or adult content
- Betting or gambling
- Copyright infringement

However, since short links are created by individual users, we are unable to control the content of every short link created.
